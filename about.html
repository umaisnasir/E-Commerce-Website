About EcomX

Pakistani style, built for modern wardrobes.

EcomX is a contemporary eastern-wear template created to present curated menswear and womenswear through a clean, expressive shopping experience. It combines cultural design cues with modern digital retail patterns, making it suitable for fashion brands, boutiques and student e-commerce projects.

Brand story

A flexible storefront foundation for Pakistani labels that need premium presentation without a build step.

Design philosophy

Near-black surfaces, restrained neon accents and glass panels create depth while keeping product content readable.

Fashion position

Menswear focuses on kurtas, shalwar kameez and waistcoats; womenswear highlights lawn, pret, chiffon and jacquard edits.
